Seinen Manga

Most Seinen mangas have a dark, mysterious detective type of plot. It's very suspenseful and many young men like the genre. Every author is not going to write a mysterious plot all the time. Some seinen mangas tend to relate to the struggle of becoming an adult or having a struggle through something.

Sci-fi/Fantasy Manga

Sci-fi/fantasy is more of an advanced setting most of the time. Many mangakas that write sci-fi manga tend to make anything with tech connect to the human body. For example, robots or human cyborgs in a city, corrupting each and every computer there is. Stuff like that is mainly in Sci-fi/Fantasy manga.

Shoujo

Shoujo Manga is more of a romance/drama plot. Many mangakas tend to make the characters look very cutesy, and handsome. All it really is is a bunch of love triangles, mixed feelings, and crying.

Shounen

Shounen usually has a male protagonist lead, but some have female protagonists. Most of these mangas have a supernatural feel to it. Either magic, or some sort of unatural power.
