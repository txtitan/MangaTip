Steps on How to Ink Your Own Manga

The Inking Process comes after the sketching and writing your storyline. The inking process of doing your own manga is the longest and most tedious part. Outlining the text bubbles, inserting the text and toning is another step to creating your manga.


Step 1

Get the supplies you need


recommended supplies:
Copic multiliner inking pens
Sakura Pigma Micron Inking Pen
(Experienced) Zebra Maru Pen
*you also need to buy ink if you are going to use fountain pens(have a pointy nib tip).


Step 2

outline certain objects



The text bubbles, the full outline of each character on the page, and anything thats in the setting has to be outlined. the outline helps the balance and propportion side of things.


Step 3

Adding detail



.Shadowing
.Inking parts of clothing that have color,
.Ink only for dark colored things, you use toner for lighter colors, or just leave the clothing white.
.Shading the background people in,
.Adding small details to objects in the background
.Shading parts of the character that have a shadow depending on the angle the character is facing.


Step 4

Adding the Text



You can add text from digital device or if your planning on what to put into the text bubble, the just sketch it out in the bubble first.
